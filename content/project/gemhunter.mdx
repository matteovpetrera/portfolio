### Discovering new places

You have 2 possible ways of exploring new places with GemHunter: FreeRoam or Itinerary. In FreeRoam mode the goal is to go around the city and the application will notify you when you are around an hidden gem, while in Itinerary mode you set your custom preferences and a route will be created based on your specfications.

### Add a new Gem

Are you an expert guide or a local and you just found out a gem you know very well is not displayed? We made possible the upload of new gems suggested by our users.
Notice that submission is not instant but our team verifies the information provided.
